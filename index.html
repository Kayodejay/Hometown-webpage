Visit Lagos

Enjoy culture, art, tourism and entertainment in this vibrant city located in southwestern Nigeria

Top three activities to do in Lagos

Visit Nike arts gallery

Lover of arts? Nike arts gallery located in lekki, Lagos is a place you want to go when visiting lagos. It is 5-storey haven celebrating Nigeria's artistic heritage,brimming with thousands of stunning works from both local and international artists

Spend a day at the National Arts Theatre

The National arts theatre is perfect for a tour of Nigerian arts and history. It is packed with the artwork of legendary contemporary Nigerian artists It is also a architectural master piece.

Go shopping at the Ikeja City Mall

Looking to shop for items when visiting lagos? Visit Ikeja City Mall and be spoilt for choices within any shopping category, while enjoying a clean, safe and convenient experience, including shopping, entertainment, and leisure all under one roof.
